Talents


     Growing up, it became hard for me to discover what talent I have because that time I don't really know what I like to do. When I reached 12 that was the time where I learned how to play the guitar and that was one of my happiest moment in life because I discovered that I can still do some thing. This is also a way for me to give back all the glory to the Lord who have given me this wonderful talent and opportunity to praise and worship Him. I always make sure that every time I got the chance to play guitar for Him I would put all my heart, soul and pour eveything I can to thank Him. I would like to share my talent to you that is why I recorded myself playing the song "When you say nothing at all" by Ronan Keating. Enjoy listening!
